How a pick gets made

Three checks, every fixture.

No pick reaches VIP until it's passed through all three. That's the whole method — no black box.

01 / Intake

Data intake

Squad news, xG trends, referee tendencies, and injury reports pulled in nightly, per league.

02 / Scoring

Model scoring

A weighted model grades each fixture and assigns a confidence score — shown to you, unedited.

03 / Review

Analyst review

A human catches what the model can't: fatigue, motivation, weather, and last-minute team news.

Before you join

Frequently asked.

How are picks delivered?

VIP picks post to a private Telegram channel as soon as they're graded and reviewed — typically 12 hours before kickoff.

What happens if a pick loses?

It's logged in the public ledger as a loss, same as a win. We don't delete or edit settled picks — the track record has to mean something.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — VIP and Pro are both month-to-month with no lock-in. Cancel from your account page and you'll keep access until the period ends.

Do you guarantee profit?

No. No one honestly can. We publish our full ledger so you can judge the model's real performance yourself before committing.
